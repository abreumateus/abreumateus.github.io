CoreXY FFF 3D Printer

February 2018 - July 2018

Improve the FFF 3D Printer made in my Bachelor Thesis in the department of Industrial Design.

Both projects are based on the HyperCube design.

The main improvements are:

  1. Correct the Z axis range – 300 mm
  1. Improve Z stability with two leadscrews
  1. Fully enclosed printable area
  1. Hidden, organized and active colled electronics
  1. Heated chamber with easy integrated interface
  1. Light Emitting Diode (LED) strip control
  1. Filament runout sensor and filter
  1. Internal webcam for monitoring and web control – Octoprint
  1. Motor vibration and structure feet dampers for noise absorption
  1. Transistor control board for advanced firmware functions
