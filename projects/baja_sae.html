UFSC Baja SAE

January 2015 - December 2016

UFSC Baja SAE is more than motorsport, it is an engineering competition. In order to maintain and improve the quality of the projects and prototypes, embedded systems department division is responsible to design a data acquisition, storage and board system installed in the vehicle. Additionally, the system supports mechanical studies to design optimization of vehicle components and real parameters to feedback computational models.

In order to evaluate the project, it is presented in the regional, national and international Baja SAE competitions. Only the best 3 teams in the national competition have the opportunity to compete in the international. The feedback from the engineer judges is very important to the constant evolution of the projects.

In the regional stage, 10 themes are presented with presentation time of 10 minutes. This project was 2nd among 24 teams. In the national stage, 8 themes are presented with presentation time of 5 minutes. This project was 5th among 74 teams
