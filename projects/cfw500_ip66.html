CFW500 IP66

February 2018 - February 2020

Substitute the old variable-frequency Ingress Protection (IP) 66 line that assures protection against dust, dirt and direct water jets - CFW-08 Wash for the CFW500 IP66.

Project restrictions:

  1. Use the same control boards of the CFW500 IP20
  1. Use the same or modify the current aluminum injected base mold
  1. Use the same or modify the current plastic injected cover with optional Disconnect Switch (DS)
  1. Allow the installation of the optional CFW 500 communication network and I/O modules
  1. Ambient working temperature of 40ºC
  1. Reduce costs in 15%

As R&D Hardware Engineer in Power Electronics, I am responsible for the product development and documentation for the stage-gate process, where I suit the project restrictions with my manager by the reports of the thermal, Electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) and Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) testings made by an intern – same position in my 2017 internship.
